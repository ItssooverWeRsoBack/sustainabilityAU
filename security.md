# Security & Risk Awareness

## Key Principle
Avoid becoming a visible stockpile target

---

## Practices
- Do not advertise supplies
- Stagger purchases
- Store discreetly

---

## Home Readiness
- Lock doors/windows
- Outdoor lighting

---

## Community Strategy
- Know neighbors
- Cooperative resilience > isolation

---

## Personal Safety
- Avoid conflict
- De-escalation mindset

---

## Biggest Risk
Not starvation — panic + social instability
