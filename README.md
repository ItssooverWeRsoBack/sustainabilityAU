# $200 AUD Tranche Food Plan

Each tranche is:
- Incremental
- Usable immediately
- Builds toward long-term stability

---

## Tranche 1 (~$200)
### Goal: 2–3 weeks base calories

- 10kg white rice (~36,000 kcal)
- 5kg rolled oats (~19,000 kcal)
- 2kg dried lentils (~7,000 kcal)
- 1kg sugar (~4,000 kcal)
- 1L cooking oil (~8,000 kcal)
- 10 cans beans
- Salt

### Total kcal: ~75,000+

### Duration:
~15 days for family of 3

---

## Tranche 2 (~$200)
### Goal: Protein + diversity

- 5kg pasta
- 2kg dried chickpeas
- 10 cans tuna/sardines
- 5 cans tomatoes
- 1kg peanut butter
- Spices (curry, garlic powder)

### Adds:
~50,000 kcal

### Duration added:
~10 days

---

## Tranche 3 (~$200)
### Goal: Baking + flexibility

- 5kg flour
- 1kg yeast
- 1kg powdered milk
- 1kg sugar (extra)
- 2kg rice (top-up)
- Stock cubes

### Adds:
~40,000 kcal

---

## Tranche 4 (~$200)
### Goal: Comfort + nutrition

- Honey
- Canned vegetables (10–15 cans)
- Dried fruit
- Instant noodles (bulk)
- Tea/coffee

---

## Tranche 5+ (repeat pattern)
Focus on:
- Increasing rice/oats reserves
- Rotating canned goods
- Adding variety slowly

---

## Rule of Thumb
Every $200 ≈ 10–20 days of food when optimized
