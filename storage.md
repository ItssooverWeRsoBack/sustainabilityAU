# Storage Strategy

## Core Principles
- Cool
- Dark
- Dry
- Airtight

---

## Ideal Setup
- Food-grade buckets
- Mylar bags + oxygen absorbers

---

## Shelf Life Guide
- Rice: 10–25 years (sealed)
- Oats: 1–2 years
- Flour: 6–12 months
- Lentils: 5+ years

---

## Rotation System
FIFO (First In First Out)

Label:
- Purchase date
- Expiry date

---

## Avoid
- Heat (garages without insulation)
- Moisture
- Pest access

---

## Simple Low-Cost Setup
- Plastic bins + sealed bags
- Keep off ground
- Use silica packs if humid
